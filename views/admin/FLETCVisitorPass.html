Terms of Agreement

The sponsor is required to notify ALL guests to bring all required documents in order to gain access to The Enclave. All guests are required to show both valid and current driver's license or Government/State issued picture ID. The driver of the vehicle must have, in addition to the aforementioned, current proof of insurance and current vehicle registration for the vehicle they are driving. In the event that the guest has a rental car then, the rental car agreement and current registration must be provided. The only exceptions are Government vehicles. The sponsor is also responsible to inform the visitor that they must check in at the Visitor Center with proper identification prior to entering the gate. Please note that if the visitor does not have all necessary identification, they will not be granted access to The Enclave.
