Story Idea Submission

Submit your story idea here if you want to spread the word about how coastal communities are benefiting from a particular product, service, partnership, initiative, project, or type of support.

Communications staff members will review your submission, discuss details with you, and help decide and plan the best options for outreach. These options include internal and external newsletters, Web articles, Coastal Services magazine, NOAA and National Ocean Service websites, partner publications, outside media venues, and Twitter, Facebook, and other social media.
