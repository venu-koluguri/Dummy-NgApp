Grants and Agreements

Introduction

The Office for Coastal Management is the largest provider of financial assistance in NOAA, offering a variety of grant and cooperative agreement programs designed to protect coastal resources and keep communities safe from coastal hazards.

Contacts for questions:

For administrative questions about office financial assistance, contact any of the office grant coordinators in the “grant contacts” document at: \\csc-s-san1b\MB\Grants\1e grant contacts

For questions on specific financial assistance programs, contact the appropriate team lead, also in the above "grant contacts" document.

Useful links

Standard operating plans (SOPs), templates, examples, and forms useful to staff are available on the server at: \\csc-s-san1b\MB\Grants

Information about current funding opportunities, guidance, application forms, and other resources for grant recipients are on the office’s public website at: http://www.csc.noaa.gov/funding/
